About Us

We are a community-driven NGO working to uplift lives in Siswa, Rautahat.

Our Mission

To enable sustainable development through education, health, and socio-economic programs led by the youth for the community.

Our Vision

A prosperous, educated, and inclusive community where every individual has the opportunity to thrive.

Our Story

Founded by passionate youth, Saphalta Abhiyan Yuba Samiti began as a local initiative to address pressing needs—from education support to disaster relief—growing into a trusted community partner.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about our work and how to support.

We stand for unity, service, and sustainable growth. Our mission is to inspire youth participation and collective action for a better tomorrow.
Our team is made up of motivated youths, volunteers, and community members who believe in the power of small actions to create big change.
“Saphalta” means success, and our goal is to drive a collective movement (Abhiyan) where every small step brings our community closer to progress and success.
We mobilize volunteers, conduct awareness programs, support education, and partner with local initiatives to bring sustainable impact.
We accept bank transfers and will soon enable international payment gateways. Contact us for details.
We are registered locally; please consult your tax advisor for eligibility based on your jurisdiction.
A majority goes directly to programs. Our transparency section shows up-to-date numbers.
